Sign in with your Google account, enter a bucket name, and browse photos & videos. Or leave the bucket empty and enter a project ID to browse your buckets first.

Setup checklist

1. Your bucket needs Storage Object Viewer access for your Google account
2. Configure CORS on the bucket — see instructions below
3. Sign in, enter bucket name, hit Browse
CORS configuration (click to expand)
Run this once via gsutil:

gsutil cors set cors.json gs://YOUR-BUCKET

Where cors.json contains:
[{
  "origin": ["*"],
  "method": ["GET"],
  "responseHeader": ["Content-Type","Authorization"],
  "maxAgeSeconds": 3600
}]
